Wyoming, USA

Molton Barn, Jackson Hole, Wyoming

Jackson Hole in Wyoming is an area of amazing mountains, wildlife and scenery.

The Grand Tetons are huge, spikey, snow-capped mountains that look exactly like something a child would draw. They are fantastic as a backdrop and are also happy to be the centre of attention. They capture the sunrise and sunset light well, and are always showing their best side.

I became notorious for lying on the ground to get a shot, trying to emmulate professional photographs I had seen with flowers in the foreground and mountains or other scenery in the background. It turns out that you need to lie on the ground to get it. So I did.

The highlight was the inspiration and information from the other photographers in the group. We had everything from absolute beginners with small instant cameras, to fully kitted professional photographers with gear that cost more than a car. And every single person was there to learn and share.
